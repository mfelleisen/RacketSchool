
 The Racket Summer School of Semantics and Languages 

Imagine yourself confronted with a Mystery Programming Language and charged
with the task of figuring out its semantics. What would you do? 

What if you have a formal executable semantics and you want to build a
production language? 

If these questions intrigue you, visit the web page for the Racket Summer
School: 

    http://summer-school.racket-lang.org/2017/

Just in case you're wondering, this is not your run-off-the-mill summer
school, and the lecturers will do their best to make it exciting,
entertaining, and useful to a broad spectrum of attendees. 

p.s. As soon as you get accepted, we will send you your first problem
set. So get started.
